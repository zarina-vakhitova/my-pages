# Zarina Vakhitova

**Criminologist · Monash University · Melbourne, Australia**

I study the role of technology in facilitating, responding to, and preventing crime — from cyber abuse and ransomware to police body-worn cameras and guardianship online. My work integrates computational approaches (Bayesian modelling, bibliometric audits, experimental survey designs) with criminological theory to generate policy insights from complex social data.

🌐 **[vakhitova.com](https://www.vakhitova.com)**

---

### Research areas

- Cyber abuse, online guardianship, and routine activity theory in cyberspace
- Ransomware, cyber-extortion, and the emotional dynamics of victim decision-making
- Police technology and domestic & family violence (body-worn cameras)
- Tourism and crime
- Bibliometrics, citation analysis, and meta-science in criminology
